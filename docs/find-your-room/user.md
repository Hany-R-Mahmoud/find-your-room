# User Documentation

## What is find-your-room?

find-your-room is a mobile app that helps you find a room in Riyadh with compatible housemates. It focuses on verified listings and trust signals so you can feel confident before reaching out to a host.

## Key Features

### Find a Room
Browse shared rooms and apartments across Riyadh neighborhoods. Each listing shows:
- Price and what's included (utilities, wifi, etc.)
- Household information (who you'll live with)
- Trust verification badges
- Host response rate

### Find a Roommate
Looking for someone to join your place? List your extra room and find compatible seekers.

### Compatibility Matching
The app explains why a listing might be a good fit for you based on:
- Budget alignment
- Move-in date
- Lifestyle preferences (smoking, pets, guests)
- Household rules

## How It Works

1. **Set Your Preferences**
   - Choose what you're looking for (room, roommate, or both)
   - Set your budget and preferred neighborhoods
   - Pick your move-in date
   - Select lifestyle preferences

2. **Browse Listings**
   - See rooms ranked by fit with your preferences
   - View trust signals and verification status
   - Check household information

3. **Request a Room**
   - Send a request to the host
   - Track your request status in "My Stay"

4. **Move In**
   - Follow your move-in progress
   - Get reminders and checklists

## Trust Signals

We show you what's verified:
- **Identity verification** - Host identity confirmed
- **Phone & Email** - Contact methods verified  
- **Move-in ready** - Listing is available now
- **Response rate** - How quickly the host replies

## Privacy

- We show approximate neighborhood first (not exact address until you request)
- Your preferences help match you but don't affect other users
- You control who sees your profile

## Supported Languages

- English
- Arabic (العربية)

## Known Limitations

- Currently Riyadh-focused only
- Host verification is preview-only (not real verification)
- No real payments or contracts through the app yet
- No push notifications yet

## Need Help?

If you have questions about a listing or need support, contact the host directly through the app's messaging system.
